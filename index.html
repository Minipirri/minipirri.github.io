Know my Work

Black Flags - Pirate Rogueventure

This prototype was developed as the final project for the Master I course in Video Game Design and Development, as a collaborative effort within a team of four students.

The prototype is a roguelike game featuring a morality system and card-based combat mechanics.

  • 3D Artist: Concept art, low-poly 3D modeling, rigging, animation
  • Programmer: Gameplay scripting, NPC programming
  • Designer: Gameplay mechanics, level design, character design
Trailer Finished

Seasoned

This videogame was developed as a first solo development project, created as part of a beginner-friendly Game Jam.

Seasoned is a puzzle game where you control the seasons to manipulate the weather and complete various levels.

  • 3D Artist: Concept art, voxel modeling, VFX
  • Programmer: Gameplay scripting
  • Designer: Gameplay mechanics, level design
Download Published

Ghost Hunters

This videogame was developed as part of a Halloween Game Jam, under the theme of "Darkness".

In this hand-drawn arcade game, you control a flashlight to target different types of ghosts and press the corresponding keys on the keyboard to defeat them and earn points.

  • 2D Artist: Concept art, character design, UI art
  • Programmer: Gameplay scripting, NPC programming
  • Designer: Gameplay mechanics, level design, character design
Play Published

Blackout

This video game was developed as part of the Kenney Game Jam, under the theme of "Power".

In Blackout, you must solve puzzles to complete each level while the environment changes periodically, as the power switches on and off every five seconds.

  • Programmer: Gameplay scripting, NPC programming
  • Designer: Gameplay mechanics, level design
Play Published

YAPA

Yet Another Pirate Adventure (YAPA) is my latest solo development project: an attempt to transform Black Flags – Pirate Rogueventure into a roguelike with simplified visuals and mechanics, featuring a new turn-based RPG combat system.

This ongoing project reuses and simplifies a previous concept, and uses CC0 public assets so I can focus primarily on design and development.

  • 2D Artist: Concept art, asset modification, animation
  • Programmer: Gameplay scripting, NPC programming
  • Designer: Gameplay mechanics, level design
